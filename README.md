# 👑 Little Kingdom

**Little Kingdom** is a Bible-based kids game created to inspire faith, teach scripture, and provide a fun, safe environment for children to grow spiritually.

---

## ✝️ Mission

Our mission is to bring children closer to God through interactive storytelling, scripture-based challenges, and uplifting gameplay.

> "Train up a child in the way he should go: and when he is old, he will not depart from it." – Proverbs 22:6

---

## 🎮 Features

- 📖 Bible-based stories and lessons
- 🧠 Scripture quizzes and challenges
- 🎵 Christian music integration
- 🌍 Multi-language support (planned)
- 🤖 AI-powered learning experience
- 📱 Cross-platform (Web, Android, iOS, Desktop)

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- React (Frontend)
- Node.js (Backend)
- Capacitor (Mobile builds)
- Progressive Web App (PWA)
- AI Integration (planned)
